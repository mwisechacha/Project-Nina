Classify Image Using Automatic Breast Mass Detector in Seconds

Nina is an advanced diagnostic tool designed to assist radiologists in breast cancer detection. It analyzes mammograms alongside tumor attributes to classify breast masses as either benign or malignant. For malignant cases, Nina further categorizes them based on the BI-RADS assessment framework and provides a probability score indicating the likelihood of cancer. By delivering fast and accurate insights, Nina enhances diagnostic confidence and supports better decision-making in breast cancer screening.
